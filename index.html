Our Story

Hello, I'm Sobia

Happy Cake Baker started from a simple love of baking and a belief that the best food is made at home, with care, for the people you love.

What began as baking for family celebrations has grown into a small home bakery where every brownie, cookie, cupcake and custom cake is crafted by hand — made fresh, made with quality ingredients, and made to make someone's day a little sweeter.

I take pride in knowing each order by name. Whether you need a birthday cake with a special design, a box of brownies for a gift, or a bundle of cupcakes for a gathering — I'm here to bake it for you.
